FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Greenably work? Greenably uses AI to scan your clothing, providing sustainability ratings and sourcing information to help you make informed decisions.
Which types of clothing can Greenably scan? Greenably can identify a wide range of clothing items, from everyday wear to luxury fashion, as long as they have visible labels or patterns.
Can Greenably help me find sustainable brands? Absolutely! Greenably offers personalized recommendations for brands that prioritize ethical and sustainable practices.
Is Greenably free to use? Yes, Greenably is completely free to download and use, empowering everyone to make eco-friendly choices.
How does Greenably ensure the information is accurate? We partner with trusted organizations and use verified data sources to provide reliable insights about your clothing’s environmental impact.
How do I get started with Greenably? Download the app, create a free account, and start scanning your wardrobe to begin your sustainability journey.
